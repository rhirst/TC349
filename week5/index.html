About Ryan

I am Ryan Hirst, a student, gamer, soccer enthusiast, and amateur cook from Kalamazoo, Michigan. I'm currently studying and living in East Lansing, MI, where I'm attending Michigan State University and majoring in Media & Information along with a minor in Computer Science.
School usually keeps me pretty busy, but the free time I get is usually filled with whatever book I'm currently engrossed in (right now it's The Fall by Albert Camus).
